Our Mission

​NOVA Debate Camp is a nonprofit debate camp run by experienced high school debaters from all across the country. The camp offers an excellent experience to students of every skill level. We are honored to offer a camp that is not only highly educational, but also affordable for everyone. Our goal is to get students together to create the best community possible.

Description

To ensure the safety of all students and staff, the NOVA Debate Summer Camp will be online this year! To ensure that the camp is affordable, the camp will be free; however, donations are encouraged and all proceeds will go to the DC Urban Debate League.
